Welcome to Storm Seek!


A storm has gripped the land and you must stay in its shrinking eye... all while hiding from the "seekers"!

In this game, the host (you) creates a game you can play with your friends in your town or your area. You need to stay within the circles and they are shrinking to zero. When you see a red circle you need to move to it before the time runs out.

As a host, when you start, you get to pick an area (in real life) to play in. You can make up your own rules if you want!

The storm decreases health. Hearts give you health. There is no max limit to health. All players see heart-locations (including seekers). When you run out of life you are "Eliminated"

    Rules: (suggestion)

    - You play Hide and seek and one or more player is seeker


    - Optional: Anyone who is eliminated by the storm is now also a seeker. OR, only one person is seeker and you can just agree that everyone move with the storm until you all meet.


    If many people are seekers you need to make them identifiable (like a ribbon or hat or something).


    - When everyone arrives at the storms by the end of the game seeker(s) looks at the notes and sees who lasted longest. The one found last wins.


    You can see the elimination time and how many storms you have survived at the end. This way you know who lasted longest. If someone has survived fewer storms, they might have cheated by reloading the page.
