Welcome to Storm Tag!


In this game, the host (you) creates a game you can play with your friends in your town or your area. When you start, you get to pick an area (in real life) to play in. You can make up your own rules if you want! The storm decreases life. Hearts give you life. There is no max limit to life. All players see heart-locations (including catchers). When you run out of life you are "Eliminated"
