Welcome to Falling World!


The ground is about to vanish.

In this game, the host creates a real-life play area (a rectangle). Inside it is a grid. Some cells will turn red as a warning, then they become holes. If you step into a hole, you use your jet pack, but your jetpack fuel drains fast.

Your goal: survive to the end by moving smart, watching the red warnings, and collecting fuel pickups. You can play solo or with friends. Everyone sees the same falling pattern and fuel.

    Rules:

    - Play by yourself or with others

    - Stay inside the rectangle (outside drains fuel).

    - Red cells are warnings: they will fall and become holes

    - Holes drain fuel quickly. Reach safe cells to survive

    - Pick up fuel to refill your jetpack and keep going

    - See how long you can survive, or who can survive the longest.
